# **App Name**: GreenPulse Bharat AI

## Core Features:

- Live Carbon Dashboard: Display the real-time Continuous Carbon Index (CCI) and other key carbon emission metrics in an interactive dashboard.
- Active Logistics Tracking: Visually track all ongoing shipments and their dynamic, per-unit carbon footprint in real-time.
- Real-time Anomaly Alerting: Deliver immediate notifications for detected environmental anomalies, such as emission spikes, route deviations, or potential policy violations.
- AI Compliance Assistant: A chat interface allowing users to query the compliance status of entities or shipments, receiving explainable, policy-backed answers from a RAG tool based on live and document data.
- Predictive Carbon Risk Radar: A tool that forecasts potential carbon emission spikes or non-compliance events in industrial zones or supply chains using streaming data.
- Sustainable Impact Visualization: Calculate and display estimated fuel optimization savings and the equivalent environmental impact (e.g., trees saved, ESG score improvement).
- Dynamic Policy Knowledge Base: Automatically ingest, index, and continuously update government policies, carbon norms, and compliance manuals to feed the real-time RAG operations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, intelligent emerald green (#1A966B), conveying growth and technological sophistication. This color anchors the UI with a professional, yet nature-inspired, feel.
- Background color: A very dark, almost charcoal base with a subtle green tint (#161C1A), providing a sophisticated backdrop that enhances readability of data and metrics.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear green-cyan (#4DF57A) for highlights, alerts, and interactive elements, designed to draw attention to critical information and maintain a connection to ecological vitality.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, techy, and scientific aesthetic, aligning with the AI and real-time intelligence theme.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its high legibility and neutral, objective appearance, ideal for presenting data clearly and efficiently.
- Utilize modern, clean, and vector-based icons that precisely represent environmental sustainability, data metrics, logistics, and alert signals. Icons should be easily understandable at a glance.
- Adopt a modular dashboard layout focused on efficient data visualization. Prioritize clear hierarchical information presentation for real-time metrics, alerts, and conversational interfaces.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth, and swift animations for real-time data updates, chart transitions, and alert notifications, providing immediate visual feedback without distraction.
